How to get a link from Google Drive

1 · Upload your image into the folder (drag-drop, or New → File upload).

2 · Copy the link. Right-click the uploaded file → Copy link. You’ll get something like:

https://drive.google.com/file/d/1AbC…xyz/view?usp=sharing

3 · Convert it to a direct link — paste it in the box below and tap Convert:
